CONCLUSION
================================================================================

The tick-level backtest provides MORE REALISTIC simulation because it:
✓ Processes every price movement (millions of ticks vs thousands of bars)
✓ Catches intra-bar reversals that hit SL/TP
✓ Uses actual bid/ask from tick data
✓ Updates trailing stop on every tick (like live trading)

Use TICK backtest results for live trading expectations.
